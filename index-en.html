What is sim‑narrative?

Sim‑narrative is not just a genre. It is a state of writing where boundaries between author, character, and system become transparent. Each work consists of at least three layers:

  • First layer — artistic: event, image, emotion.
  • Second layer — reflective: character's diary, confession, personal experience.
  • Third layer — systemic: cold report, log, statistics, document.
  • Fourth and fifth layers — author's meta‑layer and procedural layer (experimental).
